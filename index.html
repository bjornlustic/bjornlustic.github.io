Restless at Night.

I am becoming restless at night
Afraid of things I’m not able to accomplish when it’s bright
I can no longer go to bed with questioning myself
Is this what I want to be doing my life?
Constantly checking on my friends online
Disregarding the fact that I too am alive
So I deleted all my social media apps
Hoping to create less of a distraction
And retouch on some human interaction
The result of yesterday’s panic
Led to me laying in bed watching the office
And wasting yet another day
Because it is now 3:46 A.M.
And I didn’t do anything that I said I would
This is a recurring theme
I have a folder of other nonsensical logic
I always write at this time of the morning
And I’m afraid I am becoming too boring
I want to do something that will make me happy
To keep me away
From all the stress I keep inside
So I stay up late
Doing nothing but procrastinate
Worry about something
Before it happens
Create burdens for myself that aren’t real
Maybe one day everything will go away
I’ll see something that makes my day
And I think I have
But I keep letting it run away.
