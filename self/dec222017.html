FLUME

12/22/17

Check out this article Time just put out about Flume.

I don't want to put this guy on a pedastal, but he is so far ahead in the music industry that I don't know how long it will take for everyone to catch up on what he is doing. Most of the music now is saturated to pop, we are in a dip in musical creativiity. But, at the same time, songs that aren't popular are starting to branch out more and more. With computers, we either have the ability to copy and paste everything, or we can start from scratch and draw from things that inspire us. I hope more and more people begin to realize that, for any art, it's not about the numbers, it's about the feeling. If it doesn't feel good, don't do it, but at the same time, feeling good and being uncomfortable are two different types of feelings.

-Bjorn
