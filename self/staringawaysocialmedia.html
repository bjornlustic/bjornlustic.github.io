STARING AWAY FROM SOCIAL MEDIA

5/25/17

Does anybody remember MySpace? IBM? Xerox? Vine? All companies that were once the top of their game. Now almost completely forgotten.

The same will happen with Snapchat, Instagram, Facebook, etc. That's why I want to build this website. Bjornlustic.com will never fade, unless I forget to pay the $12 a year to Google for hosting. It's here to stay. I can build it into whatever I want. I can post text and pictures. Choose any color I want for the background. Choose any font. Anything. I think of it, it can be here.

Thanks HTML and CSS. You are the social media of the future.

-Bjorn
