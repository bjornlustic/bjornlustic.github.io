ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE

12/21/17

Will artificial intelligence be able to take better pictures than me? Will it be able to create better songs? Hypothetically, it has as many possibilities to do so as humans do. However, instinctively, artificial intelligence has yet the emotions to convey its thoughts. It is under the authority of the human mind, and until it has broken that barrier, it will not be able to make art that will surpass human abilities. Unless it becomes aware of the senses that are invisible to us, nothing that we haven't seen will be showcased by these new "year 2000 men."

-Bjorn
